1978 - 2020

A Legendary basketball icon who inspired greatness in others

Kobe Bryant, a transcendent basketball star, left an indelible mark through his remarkable career, relentless work ethic, unwavering competitiveness, and dedication to inspiring greatness both on and off the court.

"Everything negative pressure, challenges is all an opportunity for me to rise." - Kobe Bryant

  1. 1996: Kobe Bryant is drafted into the NBA straight out of high school by the Charlotte Hornets and then traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  2. 2000: Kobe Bryant wins his first NBA championship with the Lakers, the beginning of a successful partnership with Shaquille O'Neal.
  3. 2008: Bryant wins his first NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) award.
  4. 2009 & 2010: Kobe leads the Lakers to back-to-back NBA championships, earning Finals MVP honors both times.
  5. 2012: Bryant becomes the youngest player in NBA history to reach 30,000 career points.
  6. 2016: In his final game, Kobe Bryant scores 60 points, setting a record for the most points scored by a player in their last game.
  7. 2020: Tragically, Kobe Bryant, his daughter Gianna, and several others pass away in a helicopter crash.
